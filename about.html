The Meticulous design Behind Skybyte

The major advantage with skybyte drone sis that it can be remote controlled. Once you set in a coordinate and route how you want it to navigate using it's onboard sensor it calculates trajectories, detects objects and best of all it cuts through air reducing collision between objects.
