About Us

What is the secret behind our Drone Design

Established in 2020 Skybyte is a drone technology startup specializing in aerial photography and mapping solutions for creative and commercial use Skybyte drones have lifted off to new heights in the machine market with aerial filming, aerial navigation and image capturing, aerial racing and even being used for product deliveries. The major advantage with skybyte drone sis that it can be remote controlled. Once you set in a coordinate and route how you want it to navigate using it's onboard sensor it calculates trajectories, detects objects and best of all it cuts through air reducing collision between objects.
